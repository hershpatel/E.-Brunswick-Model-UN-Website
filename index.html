Hi. Welcome to EBMUNC!

Dear Delegates & Advisors,

    On behalf of all those involved with East Brunswick Model United Nations, we would like to welcome you to our first ever one-day conference, EBMUNC on March 28, 2015!

    Inspired by the spirit of cooperation and affiliated with experienced educators at the Institute for Domestic and International Affairs (IDIA), EBMUNC will offer an excellent conference experience with a focus on training and education. All high school delegates are welcome to attend, and novice delegates will especially benefit from this experience.

    EBMUNC will feature six different and interesting committees, all derived from our theme of Democracy in the Modern World. SpecPol and SOCHUM will be traditional GA committees, featuring a wide variety of debate from various nations. UNHCR and CSTD will be our Economic and Social Councils, discussing similar issues in a more intimate environment. Our regional body, the European Commission, will focus on changing the foundations of the European Union amidst debt crises, while our Crisis Committee, the Indian Cabinet, 2015 will deal with issues facing the worldâ€™s largest democracy. These committees will not only educate our delegates, but challenge them to discuss controversial issues while compromising, focusing on the key aspects of negotiation and diplomacy. For additional information regarding these committees, please contact us at ebmunc@gmail.com!
