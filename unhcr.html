    Since its conception in 1950, the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees has played a vital role in history such as by helping Rwandan and Somalian refugees. The committee aims to protect refugees by giving them asylum, or providing humanitarian aid and it currently operates in all major corners of the world. The topic that will be addressed in the committee is the rights of refugees.

    The rights of refugees is an important topic and is one that is extremely relevant today. Refugees will always exist and they need to be given a greater degree of rights compared to what they are given today. Every type of nation needs to play an active role in addressing this problem. Poorer nations will have to find a way to allocate what resources they have to increase the rights of refugees while richer nations will have to find a way to swiftly address the problem, while being a model for other nations. We urge you to consider your nation’s policy, financial situation, and willingness to help refugees when finding unique solutions to this big problem.
