However, he sacrificed this perfect life to lead our world into prosperity. He is hoping to meet brilliant minds that will be able to fuse together to create a super human and solve our society’s problem. He looks forward to meeting with you all.

    We, Europeans, have some of the riches cultures and proudest people on the face of the planet. We were once the only movers and shakers in the world, spreading our wealth and knowledge to all people. However, times have changed. With the Americans thriving in the west and the communists rising in the east, each of the European countries are now at risk from being caught up in the rapid push forward. With internal squabbles holding us back from matching the large scale superpower economies, we must come together and put aside our differences for a stronger Europe.

    Each of you will be a sovereign country with your own interests and resources. However in order to come up with a working solution, we must learn to compromise and work together. Some major issues we must address are international trade, national sovereignty, and government structure. I expect only the brightest minds at this summit so come with all the knowledge and expertise acquirable. Hundreds of millions of people have put their trust in you to represent them well to create a new future.

    Lets get to work!
