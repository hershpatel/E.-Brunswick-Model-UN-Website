    The Special Political and Decolonization Committee, commonly known as SPECPOL, has long dealt with a variety of subjects related to peacekeeping and human rights. Representing your nation, you will be charged with the role of addressing the Kurdish nationalist movement that aims to achieve self-governance and possibly an independent Kurdish state.

    The Kurdish people are the largest ethnic group without their own state. However, after the fall of the Ottoman Empire, the Kurds lost their self-governance, and their attempt at creating their own state failed. Currently, most of the Kurdish people around the world are concentrated in “Kurdistan,” which is a stretch of land in Iran, Iraq, Turkey, and Syria. While debating about the Kurdish nationalistic movement, the delegates of SpecPol will need to take into account the Kurdish quest for self-determination, as well as the sovereignty of the nations where the Kurdish now reside.
