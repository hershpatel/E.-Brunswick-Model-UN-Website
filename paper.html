Paper Structure
Section lengths are approximations

Introduction (⅕ of the page)
Begin with an attention getter that encompasses the severity of the issue at hand (use a statistic, quotation, profound statement, etc.)
Conclude with thesis: state what your country believes to be the most effective solutions to the problem

Country Policy and Background (⅕ of the page)
Discuss how your country has dealt with the problem in the past as well as how their general stance on the issue today

Solutions (1-2) (⅗ of the page)
Think of the best solutions to your topic and explain them succinctly
Pick solutions that address the topic while also something your nation would back
Keep in mind the jurisdiction of the UN when determining what actions the UN can legally take
