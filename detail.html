What?

EBMUNC is a Model United Nations Conference. The cost per delegate part of a delegation will be $25 while the cost of an individual delegate is $30. For more information, please read over our policies & procedures.

Who & When?

EBMUNC will take place on March 28th, 2015 from 7:30am to 5:00pm. The conference is open to all students in grades 9 through 12.

Where?

EBMUNC will be held at East Brunswick High School, at 380 Cranbury Road, East Brunswick, NJ 08816. Please use the main entrance to enter the building. An EBMUNC Staffer will be there to guide you from there!

Conference Schedule

7:30am - 8:30am Check In
8:30am - 9:00am Opening Ceremonies
9:00am - 12:00pm Committee Session 1
12:00pm - 1:00pm Lunch Break
1:00pm - 4:30pm Committee Session 2
4:30pm - 5:00pm Closing Ceremonies
